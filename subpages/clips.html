Writing

Maryland is seeing an increase in precipitation, sea level and flooding -Capital News Service

This article is about the rises in precipitation and flooding in Maryland. To confirm our hypotheses regarding these increases, we analyzed data from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration.


Data shows Maryland General Assembly is becoming more representative of state -Capital News Service

This article compares demographics of the Maryland General Assembly to that of the state to see how they are and aren’t representative. To do this, we analyzed data from the legislature and the U.S. Census Bureau.


Maryland cities surpass national average of mail-in ballots in local elections -Capital News Service

This article compares Maryland local election voting methods to the rest of the U.S. To make comparisons, we analyzied data from various local Maryland cities and the Massachusetts Institute of Technology Election Data and Science Lab.


University of Maryland students raise concerns about grading this semester -Her Campus

I researched and reported on the university’s handling of grading during the COVID-19 pandemic and students’ reactions to their solutions.


Marathon Deli Moves to Route 1 -Her Campus

I researched and reported on Marathon Deli’s successful move to a location on the main road of College Park.

Data

I've only just begun exploring the infinite world of data journalism, but I feel like it's the perfect combination of creativity and logic for me.

I am currently a data reporter for the Howard Center for Investigative Journalism where I've:

  • Created an automated system to batch send hundreds of FOIA requests, using R and Github Actions
  • Collaborated with reporters to identify patterns and test out hypotheses using data analysis
  • Learned how to use regular expressions for cleaning data in R
  • Created visualizations of our data to highlight significant patterns

Here are some other projects I've worked on.
